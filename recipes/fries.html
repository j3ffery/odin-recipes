Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions

  1. Cut potatoes in Â½-inch wide strips. Place in a large bowl filled with warm water and soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Pour oil in a Dutch oven and heat over medium-high heat until it reaches 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with paper towels and set a wire rack on top.
  3. Return fries to the hot oil and cook until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
